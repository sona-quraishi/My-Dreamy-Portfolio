Arts form my heart

A world of art flows inside me; a world that comes to life with thread and needle, becomes meaningful with color and line, refreshes the soul of the house with the aroma of food, and shouts the voice of my heart with the words of my heart notes. For me, sewing is not just sewing clothes, it is a connection between hands and heart; each stitch is a balm for my soul. Drawing is the language of my silences; each line I draw is a story that was not told but felt. For me, cooking is not just cooking food, it is a loving worship; a moment when taste, smell, and love combine to give warmth. And my heart notes are the sound of my nightly whispers, when no one is there, but the words are with me. These arts are pieces of my being; not to be seen, but to survive. I live with art, I cry with it, I laugh with it, and I find myself again. These are not just skills, these are my very being, flowing in the moment of my being.
