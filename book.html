BOOK

In a world where people come and go, where moments are lost in the endless hustle and bustle of everyday life, I have found a place to take refuge; somewhere between the living pages of books. Where words breathe, emotions flow, and every word is a light in the darkness of my weary mind. Books are not just writings between two volumes for me. They are my silent but faithful companions; companions who do not judge me, talk to me, calm me down, and sometimes become a fire to light my silent heart. Each book is a bridge between me and another world; a world in which I rediscover myself, believe, and create.
In the midst of the storylines, I lose myself and there I am reborn. I want a house that smells like books, that makes the sound of pages turning, that has a story to tell in every corner.
